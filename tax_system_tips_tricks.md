### 1. Filing & General Strategy Tips (Everyone)
- **Always file voluntarily** — even if not mandatory (single employee, one job). Most get refunds. Deadline: 31 July 2027 for 2026 (or later with advisor). Voluntary filings allowed up to 4 years back (e.g., 2023–2026 at once).  
  *Basis*: §46 EStG + voluntary right. *Savings*: Often €800–2,000. *Advisor*: Default “File now” button + multi-year import.  
  Sources: BMF, Finanztip, WISO Steuer tips, Reddit consensus.

- **Apply for Lohnsteuerermäßigung** (tax class adjustment) mid-year if high deductions expected (e.g., long commute >€1,830 or Sonderausgaben >€600). Reduces monthly withholding.  
  *Basis*: Lohnsteuer-Ermäßigungsantrag. *Advisor*: Simulate monthly vs. annual.

- **Keep digital records** (receipts, km logs, calendar for home-office days) — 10 years. Upload to advisor for auto-categorization.

- **Joint assessment (Zusammenveranlagung)** for married couples — splitting tariff doubles brackets/allowances. Almost always better unless both very high earners.  
  *Basis*: §26 EStG. *Advisor*: Auto-flag if married.

### 2. Employee Werbungskosten Tips (Anlage N – Biggest Refund Driver)
- **Pendlerpauschale (Entfernungspauschale) 2026** — **0.38 € per full km** of the **one-way shortest road distance**, from the **first kilometre** (huge upgrade from 2025’s 0.30 € for first 20 km).  
  Calculation: Workdays (~220) × one-way km × 0.38 €. Max €4,500 unless proven higher costs. Applies to all transport (car, bike, train, foot).  
  *Examples* (220 days): 10 km one-way = €836 deductible (+€352 tax saving at 42% rate); 30 km = €2,508.  
  *Basis*: §9 Abs. 1 Satz 3 Nr. 4 EStG (BMF-confirmed change). Combines with home-office pauschale if no permanent workplace elsewhere.  
  *Advisor*: Ask postcode/work address → Google-Maps shortest route auto-calc + “workdays” input. Simulate vs. 2025. *Sources*: Official BMF page, Finanztip, ADAC, tagesschau, Haufe — all identical.

- **Home-Office-Pauschale** — 6 € per day, max 210 days / €1,260/year (unchanged since 2023). No dedicated room needed; just “predominantly worked from home that day.”  
  *Basis*: §9 Abs. 1 Satz 3 Nr. 6b EStG. Counts toward Werbungskosten-Pauschale (1,230 € baseline) — only extra if total >1,230 €.  
  *Advisor*: Calendar picker for home-office days (auto-exclude commute days). Combo-check with Pendler. *Sources*: VR Bank, Steuern.de, Finanztip, Buchführungsheld — consistent.

- **Gewerkschaftsbeiträge** (new 2026!) — **Full amount additional** to the 1,230 € Werbungskosten-Pauschale. No longer “eaten” by the pauschale.  
  *Basis*: New §9a EStG rule (Steueränderungsgesetz 2025). *Savings*: €200–500+ depending on union fee & tax rate.  
  *Advisor*: Prompt for membership proof upload; add separately. *Sources*: GEW, IG Metall, DGB, WiWo — all confirm “zusätzlich” from 2026.

- **Actual Werbungskosten > Pauschale** — Claim real costs for: work clothes/tools/training (€), application costs, double household (job-related move, up to 2 years), moving costs (job/health), study (further education). Laptop/desk full cost (even private use proportion).  
  *Basis*: §9 EStG. *Advisor*: “Upload receipts” → categorize & compare to 1,230 € pauschale; suggest if actual wins.

- **Job-related moving or double household** — Full transport + temporary accommodation.  
  *Basis*: §9 EStG + BFH rulings.

### 3. Self-Employed & Business Tips (Anlage S / EÜR)
- **EÜR (Einnahmen-Überschuss-Rechnung)** if turnover <€600k — simple cash basis. Deduct home office (actual costs or 6 € pauschale), car (private-use log), phone/internet proportion, training, etc.  
  *Basis*: §4 Abs. 3 EStG. *Advisor*: Auto-switch to EÜR mode + VAT integration if >€22k turnover.

- **Kleinunternehmerregelung (§19 UStG)** — Opt out of VAT if turnover <€22k (saves admin).  
  *Advisor*: Turnover check + warning.

- **Depreciation & investments** — Buy assets before 31 Dec for full-year write-off (or 75% for new EVs).  
  *Basis*: §7 EStG.

### 4. Family & Children Tips (Anlage Kind)
- **Kinderfreibetrag vs. Kindergeld** — System auto-compares: 9,756 € total per child (2026, +156 €) or 259 €/month Kindergeld. Choose better (usually Freibetrag for high earners).  
  *Basis*: §32 EStG + BKGG. *Savings*: Up to €400/family example (BMF). *Advisor*: Auto-optimize.

- **Childcare costs** — Up to €4,800/child as Sonderausgaben (or Werbungskosten if job-related).  
  *Basis*: §10 Abs. 1 Nr. 5 EStG.

- **Joint filing + splitting** — Massive for unequal earners.

### 5. Sonderausgaben & Extraordinary Burdens (§10/§33 EStG)
- **Pension contributions** — Max Riester/Rürup (up to ~€29k single deductible).  
  *Advisor*: Prompt for Vorsorgeaufwand Anlage.

- **Donations** — Up to 20% of adjusted income (receipts).  
  *Savings*: Often €100–500 extra refund.

- **Health/long-term care insurance** — Full basic contributions deductible.

- **Alimony** — Up to €13,805.

- **Außergewöhnliche Belastungen** — Medical costs exceeding “reasonable burden” (income % threshold), disability allowances, support for relatives.

- **Ehrenamt & Übungsleiterpauschalen 2026** — 960 € (Ehrenamt) or 3,300 € (trainer/coach) tax-free.  
  *Basis*: Confirmed increase. *Advisor*: Prompt for voluntary work type.
